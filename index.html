About Capybaras

Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world. They are native to South America and love to swim. They are very social animals and are known for being extremely calm and friendly.

Where They Live

Capybaras live near rivers, lakes, and wetlands across South America, including Brazil, Venezuela, Colombia, and Argentina.

What They Eat

They are herbivores and mainly eat grasses and aquatic plants. They graze in the morning and evening to avoid the hottest parts of the day.
