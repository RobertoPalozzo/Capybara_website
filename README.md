Capybara World is a single-page informational website dedicated to capybaras, the world's largest rodents.

The site is structured in five sections accessible through a fixed navigation menu at the top of the page:

About — general information about capybaras: habitat, diet and characteristics
Gallery — two images, one static and one clickable linking to the Wikipedia page
Facts — a table with quick data (weight, length, lifespan, diet, habitat)
Links — section for external resources (currently no links added)
Contact — a contact email address
Technical features:

Responsive layout that adapts to desktop, tablet and mobile screens
Fixed navigation bar with smooth scroll to each section
Content centered in white containers on a light grey background
Footer with copyright at the bottom of the page
